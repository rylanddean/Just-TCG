Features

Every tool a competitive player needs.

21 features covering the full competitive loop — from building your first deck to preparing for Regionals.

Deck Management

Build, import, scan.

Everything from browsing the card catalog to building a tournament-ready 60-card list, fast.

Deck Builder

Build Standard-legal decks up to 60 cards. Browse the full card catalog with search and advanced filters — type, subtype, HP, retreat cost, weakness, set, and role tags. Live legality validation enforces the 4-copy rule, card count, and Standard legality. Deck sections split Pokémon by stage and Trainers by sub-type (Supporter, Item, Stadium, Tool). Each card row shows role tag chips — Draw, Search, Acceleration, Disruption, and more — so you can read your engine's makeup at a glance.

Card Scanner

Point your camera at any physical Pokémon card. Vision OCR reads the set symbol and collector number from the card face, matches it to your local catalog, and adds it to the active deck instantly. No manual lookup required.

AI Deck Generator

Describe the deck you want in plain English — "a budget Gardevoir ex build" or "something fast that beats Charizard." The generator sources tournament-placed decklists to produce a complete 60-card starting list. Import and edit it as your own.

Import from PTCGL

Paste any deck list in PTCGL copy-paste format. Just TCG parses the list, matches each card to its entry in the local catalog, and imports the deck in seconds. Unmatched cards can be swapped interactively before importing.

Export to PTCGL

Export any deck in the standard PTCGL copy-paste format. Tap Share to send the list to clipboard, Messages, or any app. The export is always a clean, correctly formatted list ready to paste directly into PTCGL or PTCGO.

Deck Edit Log

Every card addition, removal, or quantity change is recorded with a timestamp. The match timeline in Deck Detail is segmented by deck version so you can see which changes improved your results.

Consistency Calculator

Opening Hand Odds shows the hypergeometric probability of drawing each card in your opening 7, with a tap-to-expand breakdown for turn 2 going first and second. The Combo Calculator lets you build up to 5 AND requirements, each with unlimited OR alternatives — "Arven AND (Boss's Orders OR Counter Catcher OR Iono)" — and shows joint probabilities through turn 4. Useful for stress-testing search density, Energy ratios, and multi-card set-up lines before a tournament.

Tech Advisor

Describe a matchup problem or meta challenge. The Tech Advisor — powered by on-device Apple Foundation Models — suggests specific tech cards from the current Standard format, with clear reasoning for each recommendation. No internet required.

Deck Profile

Twelve scored dimensions of your deck's strengths and weaknesses — Overall, Consistency, Ability Impact, Energy Setup, Mobility, Disruption, Recovery, Item Dependency, Turn-2 Aggression, Bench Flexibility, Opening Reliability, and Durability. Each score has a plain-English explainer. Durability factors in attacker HP (60–400 scale), HP-boosting Tools like Hero's Cape, Damage Reduction cards, and Healing cards. An Apple Intelligence narrative ties all scores into a single written analysis paragraph — on-device, no account required.

Swap Card Preview

Long press any card in the deck editor and tap Swap Card. Search any replacement from the full Standard catalog and see a live before/after comparison across all twelve deck profile scores before committing. Overall, Consistency, Durability, and nine more scores update instantly. Confirm the swap or cancel — your deck is never changed until you commit.

Match Tracking

Log every game. See every pattern.

Fast match logging and a full history that shows where you're winning and where you're not.

Log Match

Pick the opponent's archetype, pick the result (Win/Loss/Tie), confirm. Fewer than 5 taps. Date defaults to now. Event type defaults to the last type used. Advanced fields — event type, format, notes — are in a collapsible section for when you need them.

Live Game Mode

Before a game, launch Live Game from your deck. An in-game HUD tracks your prizes, opponent's prizes, and current turn number. Log notes per turn. When the game ends, the result auto-flows into your match history — no double-logging.

Match History

Full chronological match history per deck. Filter by archetype, event type, or format. See individual game notes. The timeline is segmented by deck version so you can see how changes affected results over time.

Analytics

Know your numbers.

Win rates, trends, meta positioning, and practice gaps — all computed from your own match data.

Matchup Analytics

Win rate by opponent archetype, sorted by frequency. Each matchup shows wins, losses, ties, win rate percentage, and a sample size indicator. Archetypes with 5+ games are tagged Favourable (≥60% win rate) or Unfavourable (≤40%).

Win Rate Trend

Rolling win rate chart over your last 30 days, 90 days, or all-time. Useful for spotting whether your deck is improving with practice or whether a meta shift is hurting your matchup spread.

Meta Comparison

Pulls current meta share percentages from Limitless TCG tournament data. Cross-references them with your personal win rates. The result: which of the popular decks you're beating, going even with, and losing to.

Practice Gaps

Highlights top-meta archetypes where you have fewer than 5 games logged. These are matchups you haven't practiced enough to know your actual win rate — critical knowledge before a major event.

Meta Matchup Score

A single composite score that rates how well your deck is positioned against the current tournament field. Win rates against each archetype are weighted by that archetype's current meta share. Higher is better — and it updates automatically as the meta shifts.

Tournament Intelligence

Live data from the competitive scene.

Sourced from Limitless TCG — the authoritative record of competitive Pokémon TCG results.

Tournament Feed

Browse recent Regionals, ICs, and World Championship results. Each event shows full top-cut placements with player names, archetypes, and winning percentage. Filter by event tier. Data is cached for offline reading.

Inline Deck Lists

Tap any placed deck in a tournament to view the full 60-card list inline. See quantities, card names, set codes, and card thumbnails. No external browser needed.

Meta Trends

Track each archetype's meta share across successive events. Visual trend lines show which decks are rising, which are holding, and which are falling. Know when it's time to switch decks or when your rogue pick is about to have its moment.

Featured Deck

The Home Dashboard surfaces a highlighted top-placing deck from a recent event each day. See the archetype, player name, tournament, and placing. Tap to view the full deck list.

Player Profiles

Search any competitor by name. View their full tournament history, finishing positions, and top-placing decklists from each event. Star players to save them to your Favourites for fast access.

Prep Tools

Prepare like a pro.

Structured prep planning, AI-powered rules Q&A, and a live rules assistant — all on-device.

Tournament Prep Planner

Create a Prep Plan for an upcoming event. Set matchup goals — "reach 60% against Charizard ex before Regionals." Log individual prep sessions with game counts and notes. Track progress toward each goal as you practice.

Rules Assistant

Ask any rules question in plain English. The assistant is grounded in the official Pokémon TCG rulebook and cites the relevant rule in its answer. Runs entirely on-device — works offline, no data shared. Powered by Apple Foundation Models.

Home Dashboard

Your competitive snapshot, at a glance.

The Home tab gives you immediate context on your current form, activity, and the meta — without navigating anywhere.

Daily Streak

Set a daily game goal (1–10 games). The streak widget tracks consecutive days you've hit the goal. A flame lights up when you've met today's target.

Activity Heatmap

A GitHub-style heatmap of your game activity over the past 20 weeks. Intensity reflects how many games you logged each day. Instantly see your practice patterns and gaps over time.

Match Log Widget

Your most recent match results at a glance — opponent archetype, result, date. A quick snapshot of your current form without navigating to Analytics.

iOS 17. No account. Fully offline.

Deck building and match logging work completely offline. iCloud syncs your data across your own devices — privately, without any of it touching our servers.
