Features

Everything you need to compete at the highest level.

Deck Builder

Build Standard-legal 60-card decks with live legality validation. Search the full card catalog, add cards with a tap, export to PTCGL in one step.

Match Tracker

Log results in under 5 taps. Win rates, matchup trends, and favourable/unfavourable ratings calculated automatically across your full history.

Meta Comparison

Cross-reference your matchup data against the tournament meta. Surface practice gaps and danger matchups before your next event.

Tournament Feed

Live results from Regionals, ICs, and Worlds sourced from Limitless TCG. Browse top placements, read full deck lists inline, filter by event tier.

Live Game Mode

An in-game HUD to track prizes, opponent state, and turn notes. When the game ends, the result flows automatically into your match history.

Rules Assistant

Ask any rules question in plain English. Answers are grounded in the official Pokémon TCG rulebook. Powered by on-device AI — no internet required.

AI Deck Generator

Describe the deck you want in plain English. The generator builds a tournament-informed 60-card list you can import and edit immediately.

Card Scanner

Point your camera at any Pokémon card. OCR reads the set symbol and number, matches it to your card catalog, and adds it to your deck instantly.
