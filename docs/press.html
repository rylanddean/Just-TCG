App Descriptions

One line

"The competitive Pokémon TCG companion. Build decks, track matches, and follow the meta — all in one iOS app."

Short (50 words)

Just TCG is an iOS app for competitive Pokémon TCG players. Build Standard-legal decks, log match results in under 5 taps, and track win rates against every archetype. Live tournament data from Limitless TCG shows which decks are winning — and where your practice is thin. No account. No tracking.

Medium (100 words)

Just TCG closes a gap that competitive Pokémon TCG players have felt for years: there was no single iOS app that combined deck building, personal performance tracking, and live tournament meta intelligence.

The app covers the full competitive loop. Build a 60-card deck with live legality validation. Log a match result in under 5 taps. See your win rate against every archetype you've faced. Identify practice gaps — meta archetypes you haven't played enough games against. Pull up live Limitless TCG tournament results and read deck lists inline.

No account, no tracking, no data leaving your device.

Long (App Store)

Just TCG is the competitive Pokémon TCG companion built for players who take the game seriously.

Build better decks. Browse the full Standard card catalog, build 60-card decks with live legality validation, and export directly to PTCGL. Scan physical cards with your camera to add them instantly. Describe what you want in plain English and let the AI generator build a starting list.

Track every result. Log match results in under 5 taps. Win rates, matchup trends, and favourable/unfavourable ratings update automatically. Use Live Game Mode to track prizes and turns mid-game, then have the result flow straight into your history.

Know the meta. Live tournament data from Limitless TCG. Browse top-placing deck lists, track meta trends over time, and score your deck against the current field. See exactly where your practice is thin and where your danger matchups are.

Prep smarter. Set matchup goals, log prep sessions, and track your progress. Get card recommendations from the AI Tech Advisor. Ask the Rules Assistant any rules question, grounded in the official rulebook.

No account. No tracking. Just your data — private, local, and synced to your devices via iCloud.
